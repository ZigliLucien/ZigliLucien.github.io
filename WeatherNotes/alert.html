Special Weather Statement issued March 19 at 11:47AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

Southwest winds will continue to gust 25 to 35 mph through the afternoon. Along with relative humidity values falling into the 20 to 30 percent range, these gusty winds will create an elevated fire danger throughout the Quad State. If you are planning outdoor burning today, use extra caution and have fire suppression equipment ready.
