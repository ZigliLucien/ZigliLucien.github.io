Special Weather Statement issued December 2 at 5:27PM CST by NWS Paducah KY

Light snow lingering this evening should exit all of the forecast area by 8 PM. While most of the accumulation has been confined to grassy and elevated surfaces, there have been numerous reports of slick roads causing accidents across the region. With air temperatures below freezing now, the potential for black ice will continue into the evening hours. Even once the snow ends, some scattered slick spots may remain. Please use caution if travelling this evening, slow down, and leave extra time to reach your destination.
