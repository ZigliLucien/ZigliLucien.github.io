Special Weather Statement issued August 6 at 1:36AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

The combination of high temperatures in the mid to upper 90s combined with fairly high humidity will result in peak afternoon heat index values ranging from 100 to 105. Use caution if planning to be outdoors for extended periods of time. Take frequent breaks, drink plenty of water, and wear light colored clothing. A cold front will pass through the region tonight, and the remainder of the week will feature much cooler temperatures.
