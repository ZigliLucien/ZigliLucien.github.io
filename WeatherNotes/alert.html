Special Weather Statement issued August 30 at 2:24AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

High temperatures are forecast to reach the low to mid 90s this afternoon. At the same time, humidity levels will be higher than recent days leading to heat index values topping out in the upper 90s to low 100s. A few showers or storms could provide some relief this afternoon. A cold front moves into the area tonight into Saturday bringing much cooler temperatures. Be sure to use caution if planning to be outdoors for long stretches. Drink plenty of water, take frequent breaks, and wear light colored and loose fitting clothing.
