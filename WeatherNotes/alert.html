Special Weather Statement issued February 5 at 4:50PM CST by NWS Paducah KY

Areas of dense fog will develop this evening across much of southeast Missouri, southern and southwest Illinois, and far western Kentucky. Motorists should be alert for rapid changes in visibility. If you encounter dense fog while driving, slow down, use low beam headlights, and leave plenty of space between your vehicle and others. The dense fog will begin to dissipate after midnight.
