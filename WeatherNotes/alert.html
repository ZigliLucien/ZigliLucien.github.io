Special Weather Statement issued December 29 at 6:30AM CST by NWS Paducah KY

Winds this morning will increase from the west-northwest this morning across parts of southeast Missouri, southwest Illinois, and far western Kentucky. Wind gusts of 30 to 40 mph will be possible at times, mainly through 9 AM. These winds could blow around unsecured outdoor furniture and holiday decorations as well as down small tree branches. Use extra caution if driving early this morning, especially those traveling in high-profile vehicles.
