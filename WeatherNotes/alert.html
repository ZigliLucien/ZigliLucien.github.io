Special Weather Statement issued April 3 at 10:21AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

Due to a tight pressure gradient, expect strong gusty west- northwest winds today with gusts up to around 40 mph possible. Showers and isolated afternoon thunderstorms may mix down higher gusts at times. Small hail is possible today, even with non- thunderstorm showers. A cold air funnel or two may also occur. Secure any loose objects and be cautious when driving high profile and lightweight vehicles.
