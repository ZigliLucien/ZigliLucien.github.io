Special Weather Statement issued May 23 at 3:28AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

At 328 AM CDT, Doppler radar was tracking a strong thunderstorm over Perryville, moving east at 35 mph. HAZARD...Penny size hail. SOURCE...Radar indicated.
